The Pokémon Card Market

How collectible cards turned into high-value investments

Why Pokémon Cards Are So Valuable

The Pokémon card market has exploded over the past few years, with certain cards selling for hundreds of thousands of dollars. Rarity, condition, and demand all play a major role in determining value. First edition cards and professionally graded cards often sell for the highest prices.

Factors That Affect Card Value

  1. Rarity
    • First Edition
    • Limited print runs
    • Promo cards
  2. Condition
    • PSA 10 (Gem Mint)
    • No scratches or whitening
  3. Popularity of the Pokémon
